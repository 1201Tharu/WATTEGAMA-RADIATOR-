About us,

Since 2019, under the guidance of Amila Sadakelum, our business has been a leader in delivering high-quality automotive solutions. With years of experience, we specialize in the fabrication of hydraulic oil coolers and radiators for various types of vehicles, including JCBs, excavators, cranes, and tractors. Our expertise also extends to generator radiators, ensuring that your equipment stays cool and efficient.

In addition to our fabrication services, we produce state-of-the-art aluminum plastic radiators and heat exchangers. We also manufacture water heaters and other essential components for automotive and industrial use, helping to enhance performance and efficiency. Furthermore, we provide expert silencer repair services, ensuring your vehicle’s exhaust system remains in top condition.

We also offer a variety of products to meet your automotive maintenance needs, including battery water, battery acid, and coolant, all available for sale at competitive prices. We are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality and service. Whether you need custom fabrications, silencer repairs, or reliable maintenance products for your vehicles, our team is here to deliver excellence every step of the way.
